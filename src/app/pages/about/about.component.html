Bobs Computer Repair Shop Story and Values

Bobs Computer Repair shop first opened shop in Bobs Hudson garage in Omaha Nebraska. At first Bob was just building computers for his friends and family. No one knew a thing about technology and never thought they could afford a home computer of their own! Bob’s friends and family where so happy with their computers that they started referring Bob to their own friends and family. Soon enough Bob had over hundreds of orders!

As the orders kept coming and the years went by, they bid farewell to their garage and settled at 1123 Farnam St in Omaha NE. Bobs computer Repair shop has grown to over 100 employees and does not seem to stop growing! The shop still offers computer repair and custom building, software updates and even virus control! With over 2,000 5-star reviews, it is no surprise that Bobs Computer Repair Shop is one of the most sought out shops in Omaha!

Bob and his wife are known to stop by and help customers from time to time, but their values are shown by their employees every day! Bob wants his customers to know, “Our customers aren’t customers they are family!” Bob and his wife are very happy for the success they were given and are known to donate old computers to low income areas! They believe that having access to a computer is very important especially with how much technology has grown! They hope to continue their success and hope to see you at their shop!
