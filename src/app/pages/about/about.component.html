Bobs Computer Repair Shop Story and Values

Bobs Computer Repair shop first opened shop in Bobs Hudson garage in Omaha Nebraska. At first Bob was just building computers for his friends and family. No one knew a thing about technology and never thought they could afford a home computer of thier own. With the firset couple of computers built Bob felt happy seeing his friends and family happy with his computers. Word of mouth got around town and soon enough Bob had hundreds of orders!

As the years went by and they bid farewell to their garage, Bobs Computer Repair shop opened up in 1123 Farnam St in Omaha NE. Bobs computer Repair shop has grown to over 100 employees and so has thier services and products! The shop still offers customized computers, computer repair, software updates and even virus control!
